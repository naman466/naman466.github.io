>> Research Interests

Deep Network Optimization

Pruning, quantization, and distillation techniques for efficient neural networks. Exploring compression methods that maintain model performance while reducing computational overhead.

Multi-Agent Communication

Scaling laws and emergent behaviors in multi-agent systems. Understanding how agents develop communication protocols and coordination strategies.

Mechanistic Interpretability

Reverse-engineering neural networks to understand their internal mechanisms. Developing tools and methods to make AI systems more transparent and interpretable.

Sentiment Analysis Architectures

Novel architectures for understanding emotional context in text. Exploring transformer variants and attention mechanisms for better sentiment understanding.

Information Theory Tampering

Investigating vulnerabilities in information-theoretic security. Understanding how adversarial attacks can exploit theoretical foundations.

Small LMs with GRPO

Optimizing smaller language models using Group Robust Policy Optimization. Making powerful language capabilities accessible with limited computational resources.
